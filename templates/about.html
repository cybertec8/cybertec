The Global Arena for Cyber Talent

  • Realistic Attack/Defense simulation environments

What Makes Cybertec8 Different?

We offer a robust, integrated set of solutions that go far beyond standard online training.

Performance-Based Assessment

We provide sophisticated tools allowing companies to assess technical proficiency in real-time. Forget the resume filter; our partners hire based on demonstrable, hands-on ability.

Realistic Simulation Environments

Our Attack/Defense Simulations replicate complex, live networks. This provides users with the critical experience of managing, exploiting, and defending systems under pressure.

Versatile Solutions
